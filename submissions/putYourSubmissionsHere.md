## Project Description

HR Cost Intelligence Engine is an AI-powered workforce analytics platform that converts employee calendar activities into actionable cost intelligence for organizations.

The platform is designed for HR teams, finance departments, startup founders, project managers, and enterprise decision-makers who need visibility into how employee time translates into business costs.

Many organizations track attendance and payroll but lack insights into where employee time is spent and how much it costs the business. This project solves that problem by connecting calendars, attendance systems, and payroll data to generate real-time cost attribution and workforce intelligence.

The platform helps users:

- Track meeting costs in real time.
- Analyze employee productivity and utilization.
- Understand project-wise workforce spending.
- Detect cost leakages and inefficiencies.
- Receive AI-powered recommendations to optimize workforce expenses.

---

## Approach

### Understanding the Problem

The primary challenge was helping organizations understand the relationship between employee time and operational costs. Most companies have payroll and attendance systems but lack visibility into workforce spending patterns.

### User Flow Designed

1. User signs in securely.
2. Connects calendar and HR/payroll systems.
3. Employee schedules and attendance data are synchronized.
4. AI engine calculates cost attribution.
5. Dashboard displays workforce insights.
6. AI assistant provides recommendations and forecasts.

### Features Built

- Workforce Cost Dashboard
- Meeting Cost Calculator
- Employee Productivity Analytics
- Project Cost Attribution
- Cost Leakage Detection
- AI Workforce Insights
- Department-wise Cost Analysis
- Utilization Tracking

### AI Usage

AI is used for:

- Cost optimization recommendations
- Productivity analysis
- Workforce utilization scoring
- Predictive cost forecasting
- Identification of unproductive meetings
- Attrition and hiring cost prediction

### What Makes It Different

- First Indian-focused HR Cost Intelligence Platform
- Salary-to-hour cost conversion using Indian CTC structures
- INR-based workforce analytics
- Festival and holiday productivity analysis
- AI-powered workforce optimization engine
