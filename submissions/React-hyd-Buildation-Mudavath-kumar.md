## Project Description

Cost Compass is an AI-powered HR Cost Intelligence platform that helps organizations understand the true cost of employee time spent across projects.

Modern organizations conduct hundreds of meetings every month, but they often lack visibility into how employee salaries translate into project-level costs. This results in budget overruns, inefficient resource allocation, and poor utilization tracking.

Cost Compass automatically analyzes meeting activity, attributes meetings to projects using AI, calculates employee costs based on hourly rates, and provides real-time dashboards, insights, and anomaly detection. The platform enables managers and finance teams to make informed decisions about project spending and workforce utilization.

---

## Approach

We approached the problem by creating a workflow that transforms calendar activity into actionable cost intelligence.

The solution imports meeting data, analyzes meeting context using AI, identifies the associated project, calculates meeting costs using employee hourly rates, and aggregates those costs into project-level analytics.

### User Flow

Calendar Data
→ Meeting Analysis
→ AI Project Attribution
→ Cost Calculation Engine
→ Analytics Dashboard
→ AI Insights & Anomaly Detection

Our approach eliminates reliance on manual timesheets and provides a more accurate and automated method for tracking project-related HR expenditure.

The solution also includes anomaly detection and AI-generated recommendations to help organizations reduce unnecessary meeting costs and optimize resource allocation.

## Challenges Faced

One of the biggest challenges was designing a system that could automatically map calendar activities to project-level costs without relying on traditional timesheets.

Another challenge was creating a scalable architecture that supports project analytics, AI insights, anomaly detection, and future calendar integrations while maintaining a clean user experience.

Designing meaningful business metrics and visualizations that provide real value to managers and finance teams was also a key challenge during development.
