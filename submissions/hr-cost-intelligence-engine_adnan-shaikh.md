## Project Description

The HR Cost Intelligence Engine is an AI-powered platform that helps HR teams and business leaders analyze employee costs, predict hiring expenses, and make data-driven workforce decisions.

The project is designed for HR professionals, recruiters, and business managers who need better visibility into workforce spending. It helps organizations understand salary distribution, hiring trends, employee retention costs, and future workforce budgeting.

By using AI-driven insights and interactive dashboards, users can optimize hiring strategies and control HR expenses more effectively.

---

## Approach

I started by understanding the challenges HR teams face when managing workforce budgets and forecasting hiring costs.

The user flow was designed to allow HR managers to upload employee datasets, analyze workforce expenses, view key metrics, and receive AI-generated insights.

Key features implemented include:

* Employee cost analysis dashboard
* Hiring cost prediction
* Department-wise expense breakdown
* AI-generated recommendations
* Data visualization and reporting

AI is used to analyze workforce data, identify trends, and provide actionable recommendations for cost optimization.

The solution is useful because it transforms raw HR data into meaningful insights that support better business decisions.
